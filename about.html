Hi I am Bernice Scicluna

Midwife based in Malta

I qualified as a Midwife from the University of Malta in 2020 after which I started working at the local hospital. I value Antenatal Education as I believe it provides expectant parents with some basic information about anything pregnancy related and beyond. I believe that at a time when a million questions are running through the parent's brains, having a Midwife to answer these questions helps one feel more prepared.
